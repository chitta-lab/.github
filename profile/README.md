## 🌌 About Chitta Lab

**Chitta Lab** is an independent technology research and product development organization focused on building the next generation of intelligent software systems.

We explore how artificial intelligence can transform:

- Software engineering
- Business intelligence
- Financial analysis
- Cloud operations
- Cybersecurity
- Human-computer interaction

Our mission is to create practical AI systems that understand complex environments, reason about problems, and assist humans in making better decisions.

# 🏗️ Technology Philosophy

We believe future software systems will be:

### Intelligent
Systems should understand context, not only execute instructions.

### Autonomous
AI agents should handle complex workflows independently.

### Explainable
Important decisions should be understandable.

### Human-Centered
AI should enhance humans, not replace creativity.
